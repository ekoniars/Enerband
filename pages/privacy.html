Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026 · Last updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Enerband, operated by Beauty Band Far East Limited ("Enerband", "we", "us"), collects, uses, and shares personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

1. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: name, email, shipping address, phone, and order details when you buy or contact us.
  • Payment information: processed by our payment providers (Stripe and PayPal). We do not store full card numbers.
  • Referral / Burst data: when you send a personal link or a friend clicks it, we record the click and the resulting price change to operate the program.
  • Automatically collected: IP address, device and browser type, pages viewed, and cookie identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and ship your order and provide customer support.
  • To operate the Burst referral program (tracking clicks and price changes).
  • To send transactional and, where permitted, marketing messages (you can opt out at any time).
  • To detect fraud, secure the site, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for site functionality, analytics, and advertising. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Some analytics and advertising cookies may constitute a "sale" or "sharing" of personal information under certain U.S. state laws (see Section 6).

4. How We Share Information

We share personal information with service providers who work on our behalf (payment processing, shipping, hosting, analytics, and advertising partners), and where required by law or to protect our rights. We do not sell your personal information for money.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live (including California, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, and other states with privacy laws), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information and of targeted advertising.
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights.

To exercise any right, contact us at hello@enerband.beauty.

6. Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

California and several other states give you the right to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information, including for cross-context behavioral advertising. To opt out, email hello@enerband.beauty with the subject line "Do Not Sell or Share". We also honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) browser signals where required.

7. Data Retention & Security

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described above or as required by law, and we use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect it. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent version.
