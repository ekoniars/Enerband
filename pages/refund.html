Refund & Shipping Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

We stand behind every Enerband. If you are not satisfied, our 45-day guarantee has you covered.

1. 45-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you purchased at full price (at least $69), you may return your Enerband within 45 days of delivery for a full refund of the price you paid. To start a return, contact us first at hello@enerband.beauty.

2. How to Start a Return

  • Email hello@enerband.beauty with your order number and reason.
  • We will send return instructions and a return address.
  • Ship the item back within 14 days of receiving instructions.

3. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund to the original payment method within 5–10 business days. Original shipping charges are non-refundable. Return shipping is paid by the customer.

4. Exchanges & Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective, contact us within 14 days and we will replace it at no cost.

5. Shipping

  • Processing time: orders are processed within 1–2 business days.
  • Delivery: standard 5–7 business days; express 24h where available.
  • Where we ship: Worldwide. Customs duties and import taxes, where applicable, are the customer's responsibility.
  • Costs: shipping costs are calculated and shown at checkout.

6. Burst Program & Refunds

The 45-day money-back guarantee applies to full-price purchases (at least $69). Orders placed at a reduced price through the Burst program are not eligible for the money-back guarantee, except where the item arrives damaged or defective or where a refund is required by law.
